Welcome to HateXAI

This is my research project which I developed in my Masters thesis "Informationelle Selbstbestimmung durch Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) - Entwicklung eines prototypischen Feedbacksystems f√ºr die Hate-Speech-Erkennung" at the University of Hildesheim. The source code can be found in my Github Repo.

The goal of this project is to explore different methods of the research field Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) and apply them to the context of Hate-Speech-Classification. It serves as a case study example to discover further use cases. This page gives a brief introduction to the prototype. More Details can be found on the About page.

How it works

Step 1:

Enter a given text in the Text Input card and choose the text language. The prototype can classify english and german text.

Step 2:

When clicking on classify the prototype returns a Hate-Speech-Prediction and a respective Prediction-Score to the text you entered in the input card.

Step 3:

Below the text input card and the classification card the prototype ranks cards with similar Text from the its trainig dataset HASOC (2019) with their respective Cosine-Similarity to the input text. The card contains also a Prediction-Score and two small badges which show the given label from the dataset and the predicted label calculated by the neural network.
